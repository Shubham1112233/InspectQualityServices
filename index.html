Our Services

NDT Testing

Non-destructive testing (NDT) is a wide group of analysis techniques used in the industry to evaluate the properties of a material, component, or system without causing damage.

Liquid Penetration Testing

Liquid penetrant testing is a widely applied method used to locate surface-breaking defects in all non-porous materials (metals, plastics, or ceramics).

Eddy Current Testing

Eddy current testing is a non-destructive testing method that uses electromagnetic induction to detect flaws in conductive materials.

Stationary MPI Machine Testing

It is a non-destructive testing method used to detect surface and slightly subsurface discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials.

Crack Part Testing

It is a non-destructive testing technique used to identify and evaluate surface and subsurface cracks in materials.

Magnetic Particle Machine Calibration

Magnetic particle machine calibration ensures that the equipment is accurately calibrated to detect surface and subsurface discontinuities, ensuring consistent and reliable performance in non-destructive testing.

Magnetic Particle Machine Inspection

Magnetic particle machine inspection involves thorough checks and maintenance of the equipment to ensure it operates efficiently and reliably, guaranteeing accurate results during non-destructive testing.

Ultrasonic Testing

This common form of ultrasonic testing is used to carry out inspection on castings, forgings, welded components and composite structures in all industry sectors.

Radiography Testing

It is a Non-Destructive Testing method that uses X-rays or gamma rays to inspect the internal structure of materials for defects or inconsistencies.

Positive Material Identification

Positive material identification (PMI) is used to analyse and identify material grade and alloy composition for quality and safety control.

Welding Service

Integrated offers a complete welding support service from weld procedure writing and welder training to welder qualification (welder coding), third party welder witnessing and mechanical (destructive) testing.

Remote Visual Inspection

A borescope is an optical instrument designed to assist visual inspection of narrow, difficult-to-reach cavities, consisting of a rigid or flexible tube.

Portable Hardness Testing

Portable hardness testers are suitable for conducting measurements in the field, including chemical utilities, small factories, and existing pipelines.

Ultrasonic Thickness Measurement

Ultrasonic thickness measurement (UTM) is a non-destructive testing method used to inspect the metal thickness of ship hulls, piping, and structural steel.

Lifting Equipment Testing

Lifting equipment testing involves inspecting and evaluating various machinery such as forklifts, stackers, dock levelers, and battery-operated pallet trucks to ensure safe and efficient operation.
